Membership FAQ

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Your account history remains available, but member pricing and unlimited design storage require an active membership.

Can I use the editors without membership?

Yes. Visitors can still design and buy at regular pricing. A free account can save one design; active members receive unlimited saved designs.

Will my previous orders be available?

Orders matching your account email appear automatically in your member dashboard.

When will member product prices be added?

The membership system is prepared for normal and member prices. Product discounts can be loaded later from the final price list.
